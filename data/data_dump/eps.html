EPS 425   Anthropology of Education   credit: 2 or 4 Hours.

This seminar considers how sociocultural anthropology has approached the study of education. Readings include ethnographies of schooling as well as works which consider how schooling is implicated in modernist projects of social improvement, the politics of cultural pluralism in nation states, and the spread of neoliberalism. Same as ANTH 425, EPOL 414, and EPSY 466. 2 or 4 undergraduate hours. 2 or 4 graduate hours.
